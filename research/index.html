Detecting and Preventing Spoofed Sessions

Key Objectives

  • Maintain high classification accuracy (AUC > .9)
  • Keep trust score overlap between normal users and attackers under 5%
  • Ensure new users begin with low trust and build gradually
  • Apply automatic low trust scores to overseas sessions

Recent Progress
